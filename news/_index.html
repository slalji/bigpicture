Individualized Lung Function Predictor
Researchers at the University of British Columbia’s Respiratory Evaluation Sciences Program developed a web tool that can be used by patients, physicians, respirologists, and pharmacists to promote promote smoking cessation by visualizing its long-term benefitspromote smoking cessation by visualizing its long-term benefits
Pharmacists can use this tool as a counseling aid to help your COPD patient see the change in their lung function after quitting smoking. The Web Application is accessible through the following link: http://resp.core.ubc.ca/software/ipress/epic/fev1pred/
