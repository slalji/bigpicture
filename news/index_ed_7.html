EmPhAsIS Study Updates:

Congratulations to our participating pharmacists who have enrolled 239 patients to date! Thank you for your commitment to research and improving patient outcomes.

EmPhAsIS Pharmacies Corner:

Some EmPhAsIS patients have completed their participation in our study, and as such we are collecting their Inhaled Corticosteroid (ICS) prescription history from the last 12 months. Please remember to include the following information on their prescription history report:

  • Fill dates and quantities dispensed
  • Name and strength of medication
  • DIN of the medication dispensed
  • SIG as it would appear on PharmaNet

    Recruitment tips:

    You can pre-screen potential patients by searching your database for patients with ICS prescriptions.

    New pharmacy students can log into the “Pharmacists” section of our website at http://emphasis.core.ubc.ca/ to review 5- to 10-minute webinars on recruiting and enrolling patients into the study.

    Don’t forget to put up the EmPhAsIS study posters where patients will see them!
