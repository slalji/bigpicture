EmPhAsIS Study Updates:

We still need your help in recruitment! At the time of publication we have 101 pharmacies and 251 participants enrolled. Your efforts in recruiting patients make a big difference. Thank you for your determination.

EmPhAsIS Pharmacies Corner:

Why be involved in pharmacy practice research? We asked some of our most active recruiters:

    “I want to contribute to the development of using technology and innovative ideas to better improve patient outcomes.”
    “I saw it as an opportunity to promote care.”
    “The Emphasis study is exciting as it better fits people’s busy schedules, frees up physicians, and gives pharmacists another avenue to utilize their skills.”

Recruitment tips:

Get your summer rotation students involved! Offer EmPhAsIS as a unique research experience to students that are on rotation at your pharmacy – show them how they can be involved in leading pharmacy research.
