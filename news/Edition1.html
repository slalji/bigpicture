Featured Research:

    Physicians Want Pharmacists to Provide Medication Adherence Services!
According to research presented at the recent Canadian Pharmacists Association conference in May 2015, among 265 physicians and pharmacists surveyed:
~35% of physicians believe medication adherence services by pharmacists to be the most important clinical service, second only after medication reviews.
